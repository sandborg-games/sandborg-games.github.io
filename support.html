Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Aura, play until the unlock screen appears, then tap "Restore purchase." Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and will restore automatically.

Is the daily puzzle free?

Yes. The daily puzzle is always free, even without purchasing the full game.

How many puzzles are there?

Aura has 68 unique rules across 15 chapters, with infinite procedurally generated puzzles. The game never runs out.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes. On the welcome screen, tap "Start fresh" to reset all progress and start from the beginning.

The app is not working correctly

Try deleting and reinstalling the app. Your purchase will be restored automatically via your Apple ID. If the issue persists, email us with a description of the problem.
